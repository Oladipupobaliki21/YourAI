Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about YourAI.

What is YourAI?
YourAI is a personal AI assistant in your browser. It combines chat, research, image generation, email drafting, and video scripts in one dashboard, powered by Google Gemini.
Is YourAI free?
The app is free to use. You bring your own Gemini API key from Google AI Studio, which has a free tier. Image generation uses a free service (Pollinations).
Is my data secure?
Conversations and settings are stored only in your browser (localStorage). We don't send your data to any server except Google's API when you send a message.
Do I need to create an account?
Yes. Create an account on the sign-up page (name, email, password) or sign in with Google. Your data is stored locally in your browser.
Can I use it on mobile?
Yes. The dashboard is responsive. Use a local server (e.g. npx serve) and open the app on your phone for the best experience.
